# Origins Legacy: NeoForged

This mod is an unofficial NeoForge port of **Origins: Legacy** (BluSpring's Fabric fork of the original **[Origins](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins)** mod by Apace, which updates it to newer versions). Rebuilt for **NeoForge 1.21.1**.

---

## Available Origins

*   **Human:** The vanilla Minecraft experience. No special abilities or drawbacks.
*   **Avian:** Naturally light on their feet. Slow fall, permanent slight speed boost, but cannot eat meat and must sleep at high altitudes.
*   **Arachnid:** Climb walls, trap enemies in webs, and craft cobwebs, but have lower maximum health and can only eat meat.
*   **Shulk:** Extra inventory space (keeps items on death), breaks stones faster, and has natural armor, but cannot use shields and is constantly hungry.
*   **Feline:** Negates fall damage, jumps high, and scares away creepers, but can only mine natural stone blocks when adjacent and has slightly less health.
*   **Blazeborn:** Immune to fire and lava, does extra damage while burning, but starts in the Nether and takes damage from contact with water.
*   **Enderian:** Teleports using Ender Pearls at will, but takes damage in water/rain.
*   **Phantom:** Walk through solid blocks and turn invisible, but burns in daylight and has a lower food capacity.
*   **Merling:** Breathe and see clearly underwater, swim fast, but cannot breathe on land.

---

## Compatibility

*   **Addons & Datapacks:** Supports data packs and custom origins designed for the 1.10 (1.20.1) Origins format, allowing you to use existing legacy addons.
